Board

This is the main board where you will see all the threads.
Use the buttons on the bottom left to switch pages and click
on "return" to go back to the previous page or the somchan logo on top of the
page to go back to the board if you get lost. The threads/posts are ordered
based on popularity, with the most popular threads on the first/front page.
There can be a maximum of 35 threads (7 on each page).
How to post a thread?


Use the section below the somchan logo to post a thread. The post should
contain at least a message or an image to post. Leaving the name field empty will
show your name as "Anonymous". You can view a thread by clicking on the subject in
purple. You can also do it by clicking on the "No." button or the "View thread"
button on the line with the date and time of the post.


You can delete threads and replies by clicking on the red "X" button,
you can delete posts until 5 minutes from the time of posting
How do I post a reply?


Click on "No.", "View Thread" or the subject to view the thread. Now you'll
see the textbox at the bottom of the page from where you can post your reply.
Again, at least an image or a message is required.


Ticking the "bump" box next to the "post reply" button before posting will
make your reply contribute to the popularity of the thread, bringing it up
closer to the front page. Bump stands for "Bring Up My Post".

Markdowns


You can click on the post number after "No." on a post to mention that post
in your post/reply. It will generate a text in the format ">>PostNumber"
in your textbox, press enter on your keyboard to skip to the next line to
continue your message. Manually doing this by typing out ">>" then the post
number works too.


Typing out ">" before a text marks it in green. It is typically used to
quote a person's post.
